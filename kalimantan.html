Explore Kalimantan

Kalimantan, the Indonesian portion of Borneo, is a vast land of ancient rainforests, mighty rivers, and diverse wildlife. Home to orangutans, proboscis monkeys, and countless bird species, it's a paradise for nature lovers.

Visit the Tanjung Puting National Park, explore traditional Dayak villages, or cruise along the Mahakam River. Kalimantan offers unique adventures and encounters with rare wildlife.





Cuisines of Kalimantan

  • Soto Banjar -- Traditional spiced chicken soup with rice cakes
  • Ketupat Kandangan -- Rice cakes served with coconut curry
  • Amplang -- Crispy fish crackers, a popular snack
  • Ayam Geprek Benok -- Crushed fried chicken with extra spicy sambal
