Explore Lombok

Lombok, an island paradise east of Bali, offers pristine beaches, world-class surfing spots, and the majestic Mount Rinjani. The Gili Islands off its coast provide perfect getaways for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts.

Experience the unique Sasak culture, trek through traditional villages, or unwind on the famous Kuta and Tanjung Aan beaches. Lombok combines natural beauty with cultural authenticity.

Cuisines of Lombok

  • Ayam Taliwang -- Spicy grilled chicken unique to Lombok
  • Plecing Kangkung -- Water spinach salad with spicy sambal
  • Beberuk Terong -- Smoked eggplant with chili and shrimp paste
  • Sate Bulayak -- Grilled beef satay served with local rice cakes
