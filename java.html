Explore Java

Java is a vibrant island rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. From the bustling city of Jakarta to the ancient temples of Borobudur, Java offers something for every traveler. Experience stunning landscapes, traditional arts, and mouth-watering cuisine as you explore the heart of Indonesia.

Whether you're interested in hiking up volcanoes, visiting UNESCO World Heritage sites, or immersing yourself in local customs, Java is a must-visit destination that showcases Indonesia's diverse heritage.








Cuisines of Java

  • Gudeg – A sweet and savory stew made from young jackfruit, typically served with rice, chicken, eggs, and tempeh.
  • Rawon – A rich chicken soup flavored with keluak nuts, popular in East Java.
  • Soto Betawi – A creamy coconut milk soup with beef, often enjoyed with lime and sambal.
  • Pecel – A vegetable salad with spicy peanut sauce, typically served with rice or rice cakes.
  • Tempeh and Tahu Bacem – Sweet marinated tempeh and tofu braised in coconut water, palm sugar, and spices.
  • Bakmi Jawa – Javanese noodles, either stir-fried or in soup, often topped with a fried egg.
  • Nasi Liwet – Savory coconut rice served with various side dishes, originating from Solo.
  • Klepon – Sweet glutinous rice balls filled with liquid palm sugar, coated in grated coconut.
