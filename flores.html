Explore Flores

Flores, a stunning island in East Nusa Tenggara, is famous for its Komodo dragons, tri-colored volcanic lakes at Kelimutu, and traditional villages. The island combines prehistoric wonders with pristine nature and rich culture.

Discover the ancient megalithic villages of Bajawa, dive in crystal-clear waters at Labuan Bajo, or witness the famous spider web rice fields of Ruteng. Flores offers adventures that can't be found anywhere else.








Cuisines of Flores

  • Se'i Ikan -- Traditional smoked fish with local spices
  • Jagung Titi -- Corn-based traditional snack
  • Ikan Bakar Rica-Rica -- Grilled fish with spicy rica-rica sauce
  • Kolo -- Traditional rice wrapped in banana leaves
