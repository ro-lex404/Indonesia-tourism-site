Explore Yogyakarta

Yogyakarta, often called Jogja, is a cultural heart of Indonesia known for its rich history, traditional arts, and stunning landscapes. It is home to the magnificent Borobudur and Prambanan temples.

Enjoy a stroll through the Malioboro Street market, visit the Sultan's Palace, or witness the Ramayana Ballet. Yogyakarta offers a mix of heritage, adventure, and culinary delights.

Cuisines of Yogyakarta

  • Gudeg – A sweet and savory jackfruit stew served with rice and accompaniments.
  • Bakpia – Sweet bean-filled pastries, perfect as souvenirs.
  • Nasi Kucing – Small portions of rice with side dishes, popular in street stalls.
  • Sate Klathak – Grilled skewered mutton seasoned simply with salt and pepper.
